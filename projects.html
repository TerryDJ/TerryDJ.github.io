Projects

  • Micro blood analysis technology Whole-blood immune-cell assays | Lab on a Chip, 2024

    Multiplexed microfluidic analysis of neutrophil phenotype and function from less than 4 uL of whole blood, designed for sample-limited clinical and pediatric research.

  • OIL-TAS Integrated RNA extraction and SARS-CoV-2 detection | Nature Communications, 2021

    Under-oil, lossless analyte extraction and colorimetric LAMP detection workflow designed to reduce plastic use, simplify sample preparation, and support decentralized molecular testing.

  • Griddient Reconfigurable gradients for spatial biology | Communications Biology, 2023

    Open microfluidic platform for creating and reconfiguring spatial-temporal gradients in 3D culture contexts, including cancer organoids and mesenchymal stem cell co-culture.

  • Portable parasite diagnostics National Tsing Hua University | 2017-2019

    Portable centrifuge and smartphone microscopy workflow for point-of-care intestinal parasite diagnostics, with additional work on digital LAMP and bile-derived tumor-cell detection.

  • Microfluidic antimicrobial susceptibility testing National Cheng Kung University | 2014-2017

    Diffusometry-based assay development for antibiotic susceptibility testing, recognized through undergraduate research awards and grant support.
